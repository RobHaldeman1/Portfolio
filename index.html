Macroeconomic Drivers of U.S. Sports Betting

Conducted a 120-month time-series econometric analysis (2015-2025) testing macroeconomic indicators against U.S. gambling behavior. Engineered OLS regression models and executed a CAPM analysis on Penn Entertainment (PENN) against the S&P 500. 03
Panel Data & Policy Analysis

College Tuition and State Funding: Cost-Shifting Hypothesis

Assembled a panel dataset of 1,150 state-year observations (50 states, 2002-2024) merging SHEEO, FRED, and BEA data. Ran nine OLS regression specifications introducing fixed effects and clustered standard errors. Found that for every $1,000 per-student cut in state appropriations, net tuition revenue rises by $253 (state FE, p = 0.001), with R2 improving from 0.50 to 0.72.
