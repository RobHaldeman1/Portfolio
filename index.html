03

Panel Data & Policy Analysis

College Tuition and State Funding: Cost-Shifting Hypothesis

Assembled a panel dataset of 1,150 state-year observations (50 states, 2002-2024) merging SHEEO, FRED, and BEA data. Ran nine OLS specifications with fixed effects. Found that every $1,000 cut in state appropriations raises net tuition by $253 (p = 0.001), with R2 improving from 0.50 to 0.72.
